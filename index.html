About Me


Evan Bishop


I spent 10 years exploring career paths as varied as Industrial Sewing, Bicycle Mechanics, and Wilderness Survival before developing a passion for Web Development, DevOps, & all things IT.

I recently graduated from Epicodus in Portland, Oregon (A 6 month coding bootcamp), and I'm looking for an awesome team to build with! I am extremely passionate about improving my skills, and I have the ability to gain momentum like a freight train when facing problems!

I am familiar with a wide variety of Web and Mobile technologies, and am constantly learning more! For a thorough list, view the Skills section below. Currently I'm enjoying studying React and React Native.

I am also a certified 'AWS Developer - Associate', and love working with the Cloud. Familiar AWS services include S3, EC2, DynamoDB, Lambda, Codecommit, Codestar, API Gateway, VPC, SNS, SES, SQS, SWF & others.
