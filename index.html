About Me


Evan Bishop


I spent 10 years exploring career paths as varied as Industrial Sewing, Bicycle Mechanics, and Wilderness Survival before developing a passion for Web Development, DevOps, & all things IT.

I recently graduated from Epicodus in Portland, Oregon, and am looking for an awesome team to work with! I am extremely passionate about improving my skills, and I have the ability to gain momentum like a freight train when facing problems!

Familiar technologies include HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, Materialize, Javascript, Java, and Android. Specifics include Angular2, Ember, React, jQuery, Node.js, Gulp, Bower, PostgreSQL, Firebase, JUnit, Espresso, and more!

I am also a certified 'AWS Developer - Associate', and familiar AWS services include S3, EC2, DynamoDB, Lambda, Codecommit, Codestar, API Gateway, VPC, SNS, SES, SQS, SWF & others.
